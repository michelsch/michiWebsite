our story
We are three Stanford students who noticed that most language learners struggle with finding native speakers or having meaningful conversations to improve their language skills. The current language learning solutions might lead to an 'intermediate' level, at best, but do not really allow people to communicate with native speakers comfortably. As a result, michi was born.
